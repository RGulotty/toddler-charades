# 🎭 Toddler Charades

A dead-simple charades game for little kids. Tap the screen and it shows a random
animal (🦁 Lion) or an action to act out (🩰 Ballerina) — the family guesses.

**Play it:** open the GitHub Pages link on your phone.

## Features
- Big emoji + word on a bright, color-changing card
- Tap anywhere for the next card
- Three modes: **Both**, **Animals**, **Actions**
- No repeats until the deck runs out (shuffle bag)
- Works **fully offline** once loaded (installable as a home-screen app)

## Add it to your iPhone home screen
1. Open the Pages link in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Launch it from the new 🎭 icon — it opens full-screen with no browser bars

## Add or change cards
Everything lives in `index.html`. Edit the `ANIMALS` and `ACTIONS` lists near
the top of the `<script>` — each entry is just `["emoji","Word"]`.
